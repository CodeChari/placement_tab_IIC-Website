About Us

We are the placement team of Institute of Informatics and Communication. Over the years the institute has been a source of skilled human resource for many renowned companies. We have received overwhelming response from companies across the globe for our students. Since the establishment of IIC in year 1997, a dedicated team has been working every year to provide training, exposure and opportunities to the students polishing their technical skills for a bright future in the competitive industry. In the journey towards excellence many milestones were met in terms of quality and quantity.

With the best in class technologies and facilities available, IIC has always excelled in leaving a footprint in wherever we go. Excellent infrastructure provides facilities for all kind of placement processes like pre-placement talks, written tests and personal interviews. The placement cell is dedicated towards achieving placement for all candidates, through on- campus or pool drives as per the requirements of the organization.

Looking forward to great alliances with the ever growing industry, we are pleased to announce commencement of placement process for batch graduating in 2019 from July 2018.
